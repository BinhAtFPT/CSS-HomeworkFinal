Linux For Beginners

Why move to Linux?

Linux is free in terms of both price and the freedom to do anything you wanted. You can customize your own Linux distro to whatever you liked and make it truly beautiful. It is also extremely lightweight and fast which is why it is used in most IoT Devices and able to revive your old computer. For example, here is someone who managed to make Linux run on ancient i486. It also have high security which make it the most popular OS for server and enterprise.

Why not move to Linux?

Most applications that you use day-to-day might not have a version compatible with Linux. Though you can find alternatives, for professional it might prove difficult if they've familiarize themselves which the tools they used or alternatives doesn't have necessary feature. The hardware they use may also doesn't have or poor Linux support. And while it is true that Linux is getting more user-friendly, a lot of things may needed command line with not everyone liked.

Are there other Linux alternatives?

There are in facts other OSs that isn't Linux but is Unix-like (Unix is, in simple terms, a sort of standards) like BSD (FreeBSD, OpenBSD, etc) or AIX, Solaris. But we'll not cover them now.
